Subject: Revisions and Feedback on Seasons.docx Manuscript

Dear Andrew and Abi,

I hope this message finds you well. First, let me congratulate Andrew on the significant improvements made to the manuscript so far. The revisions have brought it a long way. However, I believe we should further refine the manuscript by adjusting some figures and emphasizing our key findings more effectively.

Background and Take-Home Message:

Rationale: The decline in living kidney donors over the past two decades has been notably due to a decrease in biologically related donors. Our study aims to identify modifiable factors to reverse this trend.

Conclusion: Our findings indicate that biologically related donors from lower and middle income tiers, based on Zip-code, show an increase in donations during the summer. We advocate for targeted programs that can help these groups expand donations in the summer while also overcoming barriers in other seasons.

General Points:

  • Please update the study time window to 1990-2019 in all figures, tables, and texts.

  • Figure 2 is particularly compelling as it highlights the summer peak in donations across the 1990s, 2000s, and 2010s.

Minor Points:

  1. Figure 1: Limit the time window to 1990-2019.

  2. Figure 2:

    • Thin the lines slightly for better readability.

    • Adjust Y-axis label to “U.S. Monthly Living Kidney Donation” (and clarify in a footnote that this reflects the median).

  3. Figure 3:

    • For the legend, use “lowest, middle, highest tertile” instead of 1st, 2nd, and 3rd.

    • Y-axis label should read “Donation Incidence Rate Ratio.”

Major Points:

  1. Figure 2 Revisions:

    • Keep the Y-axis the same.

    • Adjust the X-axis to span from 1990 to 2019.

    • Change the trend category from “Era” to “Season.”

  2. Figure 3 Revisions:

    • Keep the Y-axis the same.

    • Adjust the X-axis to reflect seasons.

    • Stratify this revised Figure 3 by race/ethnicity:

      • Among White

      • Among Black

      • Among Asian

      • Among Hispanic

      • Exclude other races from this figure for consistency with previous studies but mention them in Table 1 under “Others.”

I am available to meet for further discussions this coming week, as needed. For urgent matters, please feel free to text me at 720-401-9364.

Together, we can make impactful contributions to this field.

Best regards, Fawaz
